Attention Is All You Need

Ashish Vaswani, Noam Shazeer, Niki Parmar, Jakob Uszkoreit, Llion Jones, Aidan N. Gomez, Łukasz Kaiser, Illia Polosukhin (2017) — arXiv:1706.03762

What this paper claims

  • The Transformer architecture based solely on attention mechanisms achieves higher BLEU scores on WMT 2014 English-to-German translation than all previously reported models including ensembles.
  • The Transformer establishes a new single-model state-of-the-art BLEU score of 41.8 on WMT 2014 English-to-French translation while requiring a small fraction of the training cost of competitive models.
  • Multi-head attention with 8 heads outperforms single-head attention and attention with too many heads when total computation is held constant.
  • Reducing attention key dimensionality dk hurts model quality, suggesting dot-product compatibility may be insufficient for very low dimensions.
  • The Transformer generalizes to English constituency parsing, outperforming all previously reported models except the Recurrent Neural Network Grammar without task-specific tuning.
  • Label smoothing hurts perplexity but improves BLEU score and accuracy.
