Sculpt4D: High-Fidelity 4D Mesh Generation

(2025) — arXiv:

What this paper claims

  • Sculpt4D's block sparse attention mechanism reduces total network computation by 56% compared to full attention without compromising 4D generation quality.
  • Sculpt4D achieves state-of-the-art results on 4D generation benchmarks in terms of geometric quality (Chamfer Distance, IoU, F-Score) compared to L4GM, V2M4, and GVFD.
  • The sparse attention mechanism's computational advantage scales favorably with sequence length, dropping to 21.5% of full attention FLOPs at the layer level for 32-frame sequences.
  • Sculpt4D generalizes to sequences up to 4x the training length (64 frames) with only marginal degradation in geometric metrics.
  • Sculpt4D achieves substantially lower FVD than competing methods while requiring only 7 minutes of inference time.
